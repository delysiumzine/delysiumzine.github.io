DISCO ELYSIUM ZINE: PREORDERS OPEN!

“After Death, Life Again” is an unofficial Disco Elysium fanzine exploring the past, present, and futures of Revachol.


Proceeds will be split between our contributors and Anera, a charity supporting refugees and those hurt by conflicts in Palestine, Lebanon, and Jordan.

ABOUT

What's included in the zine?

"After Death, Life Again" is a physical, 122 page, A5 zine, with a digital PDF available for download, featuring illustrations, comics, writing, & merch from 40 talented contributors.


Where will the profits go?

Our proceeds will be evenly shared between our contributors/mods and and Anera, a charity supporting refugees and those hurt by conflicts in Palestine, Lebanon, and Jordan.


When will I receive my order?

We expect to ship packages by the end of September 2025.

Where will the zine ship from?

We will be shipping from the United States. All packages will be sent with a tracking number and insurance, included with shipping.


How can I buy the zine, now that preorders are over?

Digital bundles will be available to order on our shop until leftover sales close. Once those items sell out, there will be no other way to order the zine.
